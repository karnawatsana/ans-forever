How it began

It started with a walk home. Sana was heading back one ordinary afternoon, a few friends alongside her, when she crossed paths with Anvita. They had barely traded a handful of sentences before Sana felt it: that rare, immediate sense of having known someone far longer than a moment.

So she did the only sensible thing. Her eighteenth birthday was just days away, and she invited this almost-stranger to come celebrate.

Anvita came. And somewhere between the cake and the conversation, a friendship quietly clicked into place.

Not long after, Sana introduced Anvita to Niharika, and the very same magic happened all over again. Three girls who, on paper, had simply ended up in the same place at the same time. In practice, something far steadier was taking shape: a shared love of art, the particular patience of keeping long-distance relationships alive, a mutual allergy to dramatic friendships, and, of course, a bottomless appetite for good gossip.

That was the year the three of us began. Everything after this page is what came next.
